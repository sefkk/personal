Foretext

AI-powered text prediction that suggests what you might type next—so you can write faster with less friction. Available on iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

What it does

Foretext learns from how you write in context—your tone, vocabulary, and patterns—then offers predictions that feel natural. Analysis runs on your device; your full chat history stays local. When you request a suggestion, only the minimum needed context is sent to a third-party AI service to generate a reply, as described in our privacy policy.

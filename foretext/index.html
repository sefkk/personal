Privacy Policy

What we collect. The app does not collect personal data beyond your username and user ID.

Chats. Your chats are not stored in any database or sent to our servers. They stay only on your device.

Analysis. Analysis of your text is performed on your device. This includes texting patterns, vocabulary, message length, and communication style.

Only when you send a query, a small portion of your chats and the analyses that were made on your device are sent to Google's Gemini AI service to process that request. Specifically, this includes:

  • The contact's behavioral profile (texting style, vocabulary patterns, traits)
  • The last 10 simulation messages (Not Real Messages)
  • Your current simulation message

Your original chat export files are never sent. Google's handling of that data is covered by Google's Privacy Policy.

Data retention. Our backend server acts as a secure relay and does not store your conversation data. No personal messages are permanently stored on any external server.

Third-party services. Foretext uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini AI — for generating response predictions
  • Apple Sign In — for authentication
  • Apple StoreKit — for subscription management

Each service is subject to its own privacy policy. We do not sell your personal data.

Your rights. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Settings page.

About

Privacy Policy

Foretext is designed with privacy in mind. How your text is handled depends on how you use the app (for example, on-device prediction vs. a cloud model). Check the in-app settings and documentation for the build you use.

We do not sell your personal data. Text you type is used only to provide predictions and related features, not for advertising profiles.

If the app uses third-party AI services, only the data needed for a prediction may be sent to those services, subject to their terms and privacy policies.

Preferences and local settings may be stored on your device.
